Rules

  • Pick 6 golfers from the Masters field.
  • After each round, your team score is the sum of your best 4 picks' scores to par. Your two worst picks are dropped each round (and they can change round to round).
  • Lowest team total after Sunday wins.
  • A golfer who is cut keeps their 36-hole to-par total — that score still counts if it's one of your best 4.
  • A golfer who is WD/DQ takes their last reported to-par plus a 10-stroke penalty.
  • You can resubmit any time before Thursday's first tee. The latest accepted entry under your GitHub account replaces the old one.

How to enter

  1. Click Submit your entry (opens a GitHub Issue with the entry form pre-loaded).
  2. Fill in your display name and your six picks. Use full names as shown on the Field tab.
  3. Submit the issue.
  4. The pool owner will label your issue approved and your entry will appear here automatically within a minute or two.

Scores update automatically every ~15 minutes via GitHub Actions. Source on GitHub.
